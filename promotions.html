Frequent Flyer Program

Theta Airlines has three different programs that our travelers can become a part of. We have three various programs, bronze, silver, and gold. The bronze tier is our lowest program; however, it offers our members a significant number of benefits and versatility. The bronze tier offers unlimited MQMS, which is Theta Airlines elite status care. It also provides our customers with our exclusive lounge access. Also, we provide our bronze tier members with a $0 baggage fee.

Our silver tier program is a big step up from the bronze tier program. In addition to the amenities provided in the bronze tier program, Theta Airlines provide these members with priority check-ins and priority boarding. This can be an excellent option for frequent travelers who would like to minimize the amount of time checking in before and after the flight.

Finally, our gold members ship program is our most luxurious and delightful experience that a traveler can experience. Much like the other two programs, the gold tier program includes all the benefits of the previous tiers. However, in addition to those, it also contains 5000 free miles a year as well as a 72-hour reservation span. This will allow travelers to save more in-flight expenses and also get their desired seats 72 hours before the flight.
